    Overview

    Fishing for Plastic is a grass roots organization committed to cleaning up the coastlines of the world. Our Mission is to empower subsistence fishermen to engage in bringing in waste plastic as part of their catch.

    In exchange for harvested plastic they can receive cash or credits towards the items that they need to survive such as fishing gear - water filtration systems - food or other essentials. This is a win/win for the environment and the communities that depend on the sea for their families' survival. The concept is simple. With access to coastal beaches and regions overflowing with ocean plastics fishermen will be compensated for bringing in the plastic waste to collection points, where it will then be recycled. Monetizing the plastic is the solution to getting it collected from the shorelines of the world. We promote the concept of shoreline stewardship to developing countries by empowering the fishermen who live there to become part of the solution. Our commitment is to have a pilot project underway in the first quarter of 2019. HELP us to be the change.

    Goals

  • 1. DEVELOP a repeatable framework for taking the concept and the collection station to fishing communities worldwide
  • 2. PROMOTE global Shoreline Stewardship while empowering fishermen and their families to prosper
  • 3. GROW the Fishing for Plastic collection programs until they are found in all regions where people fish and plastic waste is found

    • Action Plan

  • 1. Launch Social media campaign and begin fundraising ~ Stage 1 Target: $150,000
  • 2. Complete Stage 1 funding within 90 days
  • 3. Design and solve logistics issues ~ storage, transport, recycling
  • 4. Launch pilot program ~ Initial collection site opens

    • Specifics

  • WHO - Subsistence fishermen who work along all coastlines
  • WHAT - Collection of any and all plastic found along shorelines and floating in oceans
  • HOW - Using their own boats and bags obtained from collection centers for transport
  • WHY - To build sustainable, grass roots, community level action to clean up ocean plastic
  • WHERE - Worldwide, wherever there is a need
